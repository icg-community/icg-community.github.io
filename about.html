About the ICG

The New Insane Comedy Group makes comedy videos, animated shorts, and software tools — bringing insane ideas to life since day one.

Who We Are

The New Insane Comedy Group (ICG) is a comedy collective that produces sketch videos, animated mini-series, logo bloopers, funny signs, and parody commercials. Our content lives on our YouTube channel, where we have built a library of original comedy spanning multiple series and formats.

From the long-running Punxsutawney Today commercial parodies to the story-driven ICG Minis animated shorts and the classic Kenny Clark’s Funny Signs, we create the kind of comedy we want to see in the world.

What We Make

Punxsutawney Today

A series of parody TV commercials based on the old Empire Today carpet commercials. Absurd products, questionable sales tactics, and questionable customer service.

ICG Minis

Animated mini-episodes featuring an expanding cast of characters, storylines involving Hades, the Ash family, and plenty of twists.

ICG Classics

Re-releases of classic ICG content including Kenny Clark’s Funny Signs series and other fan favourites from the archives.

Software Projects

Tools and projects built by the ICG community, hosted on our GitHub. This website is one of them.
