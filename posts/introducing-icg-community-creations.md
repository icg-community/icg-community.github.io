# Introducing ICG Community Creations

Welcome to **ICG Community Creations** — the new online home of The New Insane Comedy Group!

This site is a showcase for the content we create on the [ICG YouTube channel](https://www.youtube.com/channel/UCn4N-5R3p_j01f7HUYO3AMA). Here you'll find our latest videos, including the **Punxsutawney Today** parody commercials, the story-driven **ICG Minis** animated shorts, and the classic **Kenny Clark's Funny Signs** series.

Beyond YouTube, this site will also be a source of information on any other ICG projects — including open source software and other community efforts. As we grow, expect to see new repositories, tools, and resources appear here.

Thanks for visiting, and stay tuned for more insane ideas, brought to life.

— The ICG
